## Settings App

* Settings > System > Display > Graphics Settings
    * Toggle off "Hardware-Accelerated GPU Scheduling"
* Personalization > Taskbar > Taskbar items
    * Disable "Search"
    * Disable "Copilot"
    * Disable "Widgets"
    * Disable "Chat"
